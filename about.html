ABOUT THE CLUB

Taekwon-Do Academy of Llanharan creates and provides opportunities for individuals to progress, mature and shine. We follow and implement the tenets of Courtesy, Integrity, Perseverance, and Self Control. Here at TAOLlanharan, we are more than just a team of Martial Artisits. We are a family! Our students are fully supported in every aspects of their life, not just on their Martial Arts journey.

  1. We ensure our students have fun whilst learning
  2. We ensure our students have freedom to be who they are
  3. We ensure our students will all be fully supported
  4. We ensure our students build and gain self-confidence
  5. We ensure our students work hard and reap the rewards
  6. We ensure that every student is treated with respect
